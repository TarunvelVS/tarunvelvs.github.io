Diabetes Prediction

I enhanced data quality and utility by cleaning and transforming 100K healthcare records, identifying outliers and discerning patterns through exploratory data analysis coupled with statistical methods. The Logistic Regression model was fine-tuned through threshold adjustments to optimize performance. Additionally, I leveraged GridsearchCV and Cross-Validation to effectively prune the Decision Tree and fine-tune the KNN Classifier, resulting in a Logistic Regression model with fewer false negatives and an improved recall rate of 80%.

Data Analysis Projects

Analyzing Insurance Dynamics

This project entailed a thorough analysis of insurance datasets to pinpoint determinants of insurance premiums, employing Python and pandas for data manipulation. I applied rigorous data cleaning and preprocessing to enhance the quality and reliability of the data, setting the stage for accurate predictive modeling. Through exploratory data analysis, I extracted valuable insights into customer profiles and insurance expenses, laying bare the data's intrinsic trends. Statistical techniques were harnessed to dissect the influence of age, BMI, and smoking status on insurance charges, culminating in practical recommendations for premium structuring. The project's conclusions were synthesized into clear visual narratives using Python's robust visualization libraries, thus empowering stakeholders to make informed strategic decisions.

This project leveraged Zomato's dataset for a deep-dive analysis into what drives restaurant ratings and customer choices. Statistical methods helped establish a link between restaurant attributes and customer satisfaction, informing strategies for service and marketing improvements. Key insights were translated into clear visualizations to guide stakeholders in refining the dining experience.
